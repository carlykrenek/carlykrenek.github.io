VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

Volunteers are vital part of our Fur Friends’ family. Without them our day to day tasks would be impossible. Their support allows us to run a shelter where every animal is shown love and care in a safe and clean environment. We love our volunteers and so do our animals!

“Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much.” – Helen Keller

ANIMAL CARE

Animal care volunteers assist with tasks that involve working up close and personal with our dogs or cats. Tasks may include grooming, dog walking, and socialization/playtime supervision.

Minimum commitment: 6 hours a month for a duration of at least 6 months

Minimum volunteer age requirement: 16
*To work with large breed dogs, volunteers must be at least 18.

SHELTER UPKEEP

Shelter upkeep volunteers help to maintain our facility to ensure that we are providing a quality shelter that is safe and clean for our animals. Tasks may involve cleaning dog runs, dog and cat crates, litter boxes, and yard work. This is a high need position.

Minimum commitment: 8 hours a month for a duration of at least 6 months

Minimum volunteer age requirement: 16

THERAPEUTIC FOSTER

Therapeutic foster volunteers take in animals in need to give them a temporary safe haven while they await a forever home placement. Many of these animals will need round the clock care or special care. Must be willing to complete mandatory training.

Minimum commitment: 24/7 care in home for 12 weeks

Minimum volunteer age requirement: 25

EVENT VOLUNTEER

Event volunteers assist with adoption and fundraising events. Roles may include planning, set-up, outreach, fundraising, and staffing.

Minimum commitment for planning, outreach, and fundraising: 6 hours a month for a duration of at least 6 months

Minimum commitment for set-up and day of event staffing: 3 hours on the day of the event

Minimum volunteer age requirement: 18
