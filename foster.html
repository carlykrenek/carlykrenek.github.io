FEATURED FOSTER FAMILY

Meet the Fishers!

Erin and Steve Fisher have been a part of our Fur Friends’ family for the past 5 years. During that time, they have taken in 12 rescues that were in need of our therapeutic foster services and cared for them as if they were their own.
Currently, they are fostering one of our special needs friends, Layla. After being hit by a car and undergoing several surgeries to survive, Layla was left traumatized by her experience and needed a calm and quiet place to recover. She also needed a lot of one-on-one nurturing to bring her out of her shell and ease her fears. The Matthews have gone above and beyond to care for her and in a few short weeks, Layla will be ready to go to her forever home.
We are so grateful for their dedication to saving animals. Thank you, Fisher Family!
