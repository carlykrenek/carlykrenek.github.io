FOSTER PROCESS & FAQ

Considering becoming a foster volunteer? We’re so glad you’re here!
Please read through our process and frequently asked questions
to better understand if fostering is a good fit for you.

THE FOSTER PROCESS

  • Review our Foster Policy. Read through our FAQ below.
  • Fill out aVolunteer Application online. Indicate that you are interested in the Therapeutic Foster Program by checking that box and fill in the required fields.
  • Schedule a house visit with one of our shelter staff members. We require house visits to ensure that our animals are placed in a proper environment.
  • Complete our mandatory foster training at the shelter. We will not be able to place animals with volunteers that have not completed the training.
  • When we have acquired a forever home for the foster animal in your care, we will assist in the transition from your care to their new owner.
