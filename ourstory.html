OUR STORY

At Fur Friends we advocate for the health and welfare of animals in need and
provide the means necessary to find them loving forever homes.

ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Founded in 2008, Fur Friends is a not-for-profit 503(c) organization that is dedicated to the health and welfare of animals. We own and operate a shelter for unwanted or stray dogs, cats, and other animals in Essex, Vermont. To date, our shelter has a 100 percent adoption rate for special placements that include hard to place and special needs animals that come from out of state "kill" shelters.

MEET SADIE

Our beloved Sadie, the inspiration for Fur Friends, adopted us - four college students - as her forever family in 1999 when she took refuge under the porch of our apartment in Burlington, Vermont. We all remember that day like it was yesterday.

It was a cold and rainy night, and we immediately knew she needed the care of a vet. While at the vet we learned she had been hit by a car and the incident had crushed her tail and leg. Due to infection, both had to be amputated. Wanting to give her the best opportunity to live, we took her in as our own and nursed her back to health.

After her recovery she went to live with one of our founders, Clarisa, in Jericho, Vermont. She remained a loyal and loving calico until she crossed the Rainbow Bridge in 2007. Her legacy lives on here at Fur Friends everyday.
