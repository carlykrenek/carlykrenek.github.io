ABOUT US

OUR STORY

It all started with a tiny calico kitten named Sadie and a group of college students with a soft spot for animals in need. Now Fur Friends is one of the leading animal rescues in our area, saving over 200 animals a year.

OUR TEAM

Meet our Fur Friends’ team, the lifeblood of our organization and the people that work tirelessly to make a difference in our community when it comes to animal health and welfare.

SAVE THE DATE

We host two annual fundraising events a year to raise funds that help keep our shelter open and functioning. Learn more about Paws In The Park and Barkfest.

STAY IN TOUCH

Have questions, comments, or concerns? We are happy to help! Visit our Contact page for ways to reach out to us.

We are their voice. Let’s take action to save and protect them.
