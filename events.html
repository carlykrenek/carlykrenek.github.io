EVENTS

Fur Friends is a not-for-profit organization that relies on the generosity of others to support its efforts to find forever homes. Our hosted events are a way for us to maintain the financial stability necessary to continue to provide shelter and care for animals in need.

PAWS IN THE PARK

Paws in the Park is our annual spring dog walk fundraising event. We invite the public to come out and bring their fur friends to have some fun and get some exercise. Attendees will participate in a 30 minute walk and enjoy food and games with their pup. Some of our fur friends available for adoption will be in attendance, as well.

Registration fee: $10 Date: Saturday, May 27, 2023
Location:
Pearl Street Park
183 Pearl St,
Essex Junction, VT 05452
Requirements for participation:
  • Proof of updated pet vaccines
  • Dogs must play well with others
  • Signed waiver
Things to note:
  • Free parking
  • Water provided
  • Cash & card accepted
  • Food trucks on hand
 Volunteer Opportunities:
  • Fundraiser/Outreach
  • Planning
  • Dog walker
  • Greeter

ADOPTION EVENTS

Aside from our fundraising events, we also host adoption events every 1st Saturday of every month. Potential owners can come out and meet some of our animals at these events and adopt on the spot. We often offer lower adoption fees at these events. Follow our social media sites for pop-up adoption events that get planned throughout the year.

Date: 1st Saturday of every month
Location:
South Burlington Petco
861 Williston Rd,
South Burlington, Vermont 05403
