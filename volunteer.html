VOLUNTEER

VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

Volunteers are a vital part of our mission here at Fur Friends. They make a difference in the lives of our animals and in our community. Are you interested in working with our animals or helping maintain our facility to provide quality shelter and care for them? Check out our volunteer opportunities.

ANIMAL CARE

Animal care volunteers assist with tasks that involve working up close and personal with our dogs or cats. Tasks may include grooming, exercise, socialization, or feeding.

SHELTER UPKEEP

Shelter upkeep volunteers help to maintain our facility to ensure that we are providing a quality shelter that is safe and clean for our animals. Tasks may involve cleaning and maintenance.

THERAPEUTIC FOSTER

Therapeutic foster volunteers take in animals in need to give them a temporary safe haven while they await a forever home placement.

EVENT VOLUNTEER

Event volunteers assist with adoption and fundraising events. Roles may include planning, set-up, outreach, fundraising, and staffing.

THE VOLUNTEER PROCESS

Volunteering is a heartfelt way to give back to your community. Your volunteer efforts help maintain our shelter and bring joy and companionship to our animals. We could not provide the level of care that we do without our amazing volunteers.

  • Review our Volunteer Policy & Volunteer FAQ .
  • Fill out a Volunteer Application online.
  • Speak with one of our Fur Friends’ volunteer coordinators.
  • Complete an orientation training.

VOLUNTEER OF THE MONTH

Meet Alexandra!

Alex started volunteering in our cattery back in 2021. When she first started she was routinely playing with our cats to keep them busy and give them an opportunity for enrichment and socialization. After consistently coming to volunteer, she offered to take on other volunteer tasks, as well. She also volunteers to help keep our cattery clean and well maintained.
The love Alex has for animals and the passion she has for helping others is heartwarming to witness. Our cats adore her and so do we.
We are so grateful for her continued time and support. Thank you, Alex!
