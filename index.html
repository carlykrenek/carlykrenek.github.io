Our friends are waiting to meet you!

Fur Friends is a non-profit, no-kill shelter located in Essex, Vermont.
We advocate for the health and welfare of animals in need and provide the means necessary to care for them while finding loving forever homes.

FEATURED FRIEND

Max Adult Male Pitbull

Meet Max! Rescuers brought him in after finding him on the side of the road as a small pup. After two years in our care, he’s ready to find a loving home. With his sweet disposition and fun-loving temperament, he will surely bring joy to any family.

WAYS TO SHOW YOUR SUPPORT

ADOPT

When you adopt, you give a deserving animal a new chance at life and allow us to save another animal from a local kill shelter.

FOSTER

When you become a foster, you help improve the life of an animal in need by offering love and care while they await a forever home.

DONATE

When you make monetary donations, you provide us with the means to support the health and wellbeing of the animals in our care.

VOLUNTEER

When you volunteer, you assist with critical tasks that benefit our animals and allow our shelter to function efficiently.
